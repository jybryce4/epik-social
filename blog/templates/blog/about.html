About ePIK

Overview

ePik is a social network by James Bryce (on-site as Jamey). He wrote the site using the Django framework and it is currently open-source on github.

What makes ePIK different?

ePIK should be a breath of fresh air in a world dominated by Facebook and Twitter. Similar to Tumblr, but unique in style, your data is not collected or monetized by the ePIK Social Organization. We are not looking to profit, so we do not target ads at you nor collect your personal information.

Join our team!

We need collaborators to develop and administrate the site. Please email James if you wish to be a part of our volunteer team. Include in the email your full name and relevant experience. If you wish to fork the code for your own projects, go ahead!

FAQ

Below is a list of frequently asked questions

Why make a social network open-source?

We made ePIK open source because we believe that sharing is caring and we want to promote the continual development of the Web. To this end, people are free to fork our code and use it as they see fit to make their own sites.

Why should I join ePIK?

We care about your privacy. We don't use targetted advertising, collect your usage data, or use your data for anything not specific to ePIK itself. We're different from Facebook and Twitter in that we have stricter Community Guidelines to ensure quality and safety for all users, have no interest in your personal data (other than keeping it safe!), and our site is designed so that you see what's most important to you on your homepage--not just what's trending.

Site Rules

Please see our Community Guidelines before you register to make sure you understand the expected behavior on our site. Failure to follow the rules can result in account deletion.
