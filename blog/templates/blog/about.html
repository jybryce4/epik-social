About ePIK

Overview

ePik is a social network by James Bryce (on-site as Jamey). He wrote the site using the Django framework and it is currently open-source on github.

What makes ePIK different?

ePIK should be a breath of fresh air in a world dominated by Facebook and Twitter. Similar to Tumblr, but unique in style, your data is not collected or monetized by the ePIK Social organization. We are not looking to profit, so we do not target ads at you nor collect your personal information.

Join our team!

We need collaborators to develop and administrate the site. Please email James if you wish to be a part of our volunteer team. Include in the email your full name and relevant experience. If you wish to fork the code for your own projects, go ahead!
