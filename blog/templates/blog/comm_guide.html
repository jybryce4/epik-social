Community Guidelines

Here at ePIK, we want this place to be safe for every user.

It's unfortunate that we live in a world where these things have to be said, but here we are. For this reason, we have established the rules below.

Rule 1 - No Hate

THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT RULE!

Under NO circumstances will cyberbullying, making personal information public for malevolent use, trolling, hate-speech, discrimination on the basis of race, sexual orientation, gender, disability, etc, or otherwise hurtful behavior be tolerated.

Any account that engages in hateful activity will be DELETED without warning.

No hate. No exceptions.

Rule 2 - No Illegal Activity

Illegal activity includes, but is not limited to:

  • Distribution or sales of illegal drugs
  • Gambling
  • Hacking Websites and Servers
  • The distribution of underage pornography
  • Encouraging illegal activity

In order to keep the site safe and avoid lawsuits, we cannot allow any illegal activity. Not only is it against the law, but in many cases it is immoral.

Any account that engages in illegal activity will be DELETED without warning.

Rule 3 - No Pornography

Pornography is images or videos of a sexually explicit nature or links to pornographic content.

Any account that posts any kind of pornography will be DELETED without warning.

However, talking about sex is not against the rules--as long as it isn't hateful.

Rule 4 - No Asking for Site Admin Permissions

Stop trying to make it happen. It's never going to happen. If you ask an admin to give you those permissions, your request will be ignored. If you start drama about it, then your account will be DELETED.

Rule 5 - No Spam

Repetitive posts, nonsensical character strings, and advertising products without permission will not be tolerated.

If you do not adjust your posting behavior, your account will be DELETED.

As you can see, our rules are pretty simple, but they are NOT negotiable. Follow them or you will not be allowed to participate in discussions on ePIK!
